Savings Stokvel

Group members contribute regularly, with each member receiving the pooled money in rotation.

  • Use for emergencies, festive spending, or bulk groceries.
  • Example: 10 members contribute R1,000 monthly.

How Makhie Helps:

  • Automates contributions and payouts.
  • Sends reminders and offers savings insights.

Investment Stokvel

Pooled funds are invested in income-generating assets like taxis or businesses.

  • Example: Buy a minibus taxi for long-term income.

How Makhie Helps:

  • Suggests investment opportunities.
  • Tracks profits and automates payouts.

Grocery or Bulk Buying Stokvel

Group members pool money to buy goods in bulk at discounted rates.

How Makhie Helps:

  • Partners with retailers for bulk discounts.
  • Coordinates delivery logistics.

Property Stokvel

Members save together to invest in real estate like rental properties.

How Makhie Helps:

  • Tracks savings towards property goals.
  • Connects with developers and mortgage providers.

Loan/Stokvel Lending Model

Members can borrow from the group fund with low or no interest.

How Makhie Helps:

  • Automates loan requests and repayments.
  • Ensures transparency with loan distribution.

Celebration or Events Stokvel

Members save together to fund weddings, birthdays, or funerals.

How Makhie Helps:

  • Keeps a record of contributions and expenses.
  • Partners with vendors for discounts.

Business Funding Stokvel

Members invest together in a business and share the profits.

How Makhie Helps:

  • Manages business income and expenses.
  • Offers financial literacy tools for sustainable growth.
