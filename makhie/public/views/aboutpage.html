Welcome to Makhie – Turning Your Moola into More!

At Makhie, we believe in the power of community and smart financial decisions. Inspired by the tradition of stokvels, we’ve created a platform that empowers groups to go beyond just saving – we help you invest, earn, and grow wealth together.

What We Do

  • Empower Group Investments: Pool funds to invest in income-generating assets like minibus taxis, rental properties, or businesses.
  • Financial Transparency: Real-time dashboards track every contribution, payout, and profit, ensuring accountability.
  • Secure Banking with FNB: We partner with FNB to offer seamless and safe banking services with insured protection.
  • Simple and Accessible: User-friendly and mobile-first, Makhie makes it easy for everyone to participate in group investments.

Our Vision

We see a future where informal businesses, stokvels, and community investments thrive. By combining traditional savings practices with modern fintech tools, Makhie empowers communities, one group at a time.

Why Choose Makhie?

  • Your Money, Your Rules: Decide how to invest and watch your moola grow.
  • Earn While You Save: Turn pooled savings into income-generating assets.
  • Transparency You Can Trust: Every transaction is tracked and shared in real-time.
  • Secure with FNB: Enjoy insured financial services from a trusted bank.
